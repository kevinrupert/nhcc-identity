NHCC Digital Rebrand

Identity Design

Symbol

The core of the new identity is a simple, strong letterform built on the geometry of a cross.

Logo

The Symbol can be paired with a clear, personable wordmark in situations where the entire church name is required.

Style

To allude to the church’s history, a recognizable “Raspberry” serves as the ownable color. Using the color also differentiates the brand from nearby churches who apply blues and greens. Raspberry can be offset with a palette of neutrals depending on the application.

👆 Circular is the primary, workhorse typeface of the brand and should be used the majority of the time. In certain, more illustrative applications, Dharma Gothic—the foundation for the Symbol—can be introduced.

Visual Language

While the foundational elements of the brand are simple, the applications are extensive. The Symbol’s shape is an ownable geometry that can be applied to a variety of situations in combination with color, typography, and imagery.

👆 For sermon graphics, use Unsplash for an appropriate, high-quality background image. Update content in this template and your graphics will be consistent and well-designed every time.

👆 “Gather, Learn, and Serve” messaging as icons—built in the rectangular geometry of the Symbol.

👆 Symbol variations for Students and Kids programs.
